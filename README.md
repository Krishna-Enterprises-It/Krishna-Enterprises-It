# Krishna Enterprises 🏢🛠️

Welcome to the official GitHub profile of **Krishna Enterprises**. We are an integrated solutions provider specializing in **IT Infrastructure**, **Enterprise Security**, **Fire Safety**, and **Turnkey Corporate Renovation & Interior Decoration**. 

We bridge the gap between physical infrastructure and digital efficiency, ensuring businesses are smart, safe, secure, and beautifully designed.

---

## 🛠️ Our Core Expertise

We deliver comprehensive, end-to-end solutions across multiple domains:

### 🌐 1. IT Infrastructure & Support Services
* **Network & IT Support:** Managed IT services, server maintenance, and helpdesk support.
* **Network Architecture:** LAN/WAN setup, structured cabling, Wi-Fi solutions, and routing/switching.
* **Hardware Provisioning:** Sourcing, deploying, and maintaining corporate hardware assets.

### 🛡️ 2. Advanced Security & Surveillance
* **CCTV & Video Analytics:** High-definition IP surveillance, remote monitoring setups, and NVR/DVR management.
* **Access Control Systems:** Biometric, RFID, and smart-lock integration for enterprise security.

### 🔥 3. Fire Alarm & Life Safety Systems
* **Detection & Prevention:** Conventional and addressable fire alarm systems, smoke detectors, and heat sensors.
* **Compliance & Integration:** Designing building safety systems that comply with local regulatory standards and integrate with central building management software (BMS).

### 📐 4. Renovation & Interior Decoration
* **Turnkey Corporate Interiors:** Conceptualizing, 3D modeling, and executing modern office spaces.
* **Civil & Electrical Work:** Comprehensive renovation, smart lighting, and space-optimization layouts.
